Chicken

Southern fried chicken, also known simply as fried chicken, is a dish consisting of chicken pieces that have been coated with seasoned flour or batter and pan-fried, deep fried, pressure fried, or air fried. The breading adds a crisp coating or crust to the exterior of the chicken while retaining juices in the meat. Broiler chickens are most commonly used.

Beef

Beef Stroganov or Beef Stroganoff is a traditional recipe of Russian cuisine: a beef stew marinated, sautéed, then braised with a smetana or sour cream sauce, mustard, paprika, onions, and of mushrooms. This classic recipe of Russian cuisine is to this day spread all over the world in many variations.

Sushi

Sushi (寿司, 鮨, 鮓?) Is a traditional Japanese dish, consisting of a vinegared rice called shari (舎 利?) Combined with another ingredient called neta (寿司 ネ タ?) Which is usually raw fish or fruit. sea. This form of culinary art is one of the emblems of Japanese cuisine around the world, although its consumption is only occasional in Japan.
